Abstract

It is based upon Diffusion Monte Carlo with significant modifications: correlated pairs of random walkers that carry opposite signs; different functions â€œguideâ€ walkers of different signs; the Gaussians used for members of a pair are correlated; walkers can cancel so as to conserve their expected future contributions.
