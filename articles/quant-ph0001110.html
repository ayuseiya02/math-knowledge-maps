Abstract

Great progress has been made recently in establishing conditions for separability of a particular class of Werner densities on the tensor product space of n𝑛italic-nn d𝑑italic-dd–level systems (qudits). In this brief note we complete the process of establishing necessary and sufficient conditions for separability of these Werner densities by proving the sufficient condition for general n𝑛italic-nn and d𝑑italic-dd.
